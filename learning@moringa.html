    • How Practical is it?

    Moringa school runs a curriculum that is focused in preparing the learner for the workplace. Practical lessons are offered in the Canvas platform and the sudent is required to follow along and create something practical of their own for practice after each lesson. Incase of Blockers, daily standups are held to make sure the learner is upto speed and understands. Students are also required to come up with Indipendent Projects at the end of the week to showcase what they learned. So yeah, very practical.
